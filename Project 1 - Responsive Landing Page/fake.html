Interactive Navigation Demo

Scroll down to see the navigation bar's color change effect. Hover over menu items to see the underline animation.

The navigation bar is fixed to the top of the page and will stay visible as you scroll. When scrolling down, the background becomes darker and the text becomes lighter for better contrast.

Each menu item has a smooth underline animation on hover, with colors that adapt to the navigation bar's current state.

On mobile devices, the menu will collapse into a hamburger menu for better usability on smaller screens.
